Why do we exist?

In our society, students need proper guidance in order to achieve success. Nowadays, the young generation wants everything to happen within a blink of an eye and in this situation they make wrong decisions regarding their career. We at Motiver Foundation, have an android app where we publish stories of people who achieved a lot in their career and can be a source of inspiration for others.
